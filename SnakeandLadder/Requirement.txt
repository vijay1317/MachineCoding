Requirements:
-Board of configurable dimensions (commonly 10x10, 100 cells).
-Multiple players take turns rolling dice.
-Board positions can contain snakes (slide down) or ladders (climb up)
-First player to reach the last cell wins.

System Design:
- Game: Manages game lifecycle, players, dice rolls, and game flow.
- Board: Handles snakes, ladders, and general cell management.
- Player: Tracks name and position.
- Dice: Simulates dice roll.
- Snake/Ladder: Define start and end cell, and messages
